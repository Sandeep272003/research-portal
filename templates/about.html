About the AI Research Portal

This portal provides two specialized, analyst-grade tools using advanced open-source algorithms for financial research. Outputs are structured, reliable, and optimized for professional workflows.

1. Financial Statement Extraction

  • Advanced regex parsing with multi-line support and negative values
  • Extended currency (USD, EUR, INR, etc.) and units (billions, crores) detection
  • Comprehensive synonym mapping for line items
  • Handles up to 5 years/periods with fallback labels
  • Exports to clean, indexed Excel with N/A for missing data

2. Earnings Call Summary

  • Enhanced sentiment with polarity/subjectivity thresholds
  • NLTK-based sentence extraction for accuracy
  • Extended keywords for guidance, capacity, and initiatives
  • Truncation for long summaries and sentiment-filtered bullets
  • Clear "Not mentioned" for absent sections
